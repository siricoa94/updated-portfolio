Below are three homework assignments, and a group assignment I worked on. Included below are the screenshots and a brief description of each with a link included to the program. So feel free to give it a try!.

This is the Crystal Collector! Each crystal is assigned a hidden random value. You must match your total to the number displayed in order to win. Click the crystals to increase your numebr, but make sure not to go too high or you will lose. Crystal Collector!

This is the Andrew's Silly Riddle Quiz. Just like the title suggests, it is a silly riddle quiz. Although not all the questions are riddles, they will prove tobe challenging. Answer all ten before the timer runs out and see how well you do at the end when the score is displayed. Want to play another round? simply hit the reset button! Andrew's Silly Quiz

This is the Movie Cheat Sheet App. This program is designed to deliver Movie or Television information relevant to a user search. Along with a gify, a poster will appear for anything related to the search as well as ratings, cast, and other information for specified movies or shows. Movie Cheat Sheet
